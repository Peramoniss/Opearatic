About

This site is a personal project of mine (Eduardo Pereira) to susbtitute the manual list-making process I had in RYM, since I am constantly updating it and it's a drag to keep consistency like that. The tone is informal and the idea is just to gather my taste in music in a nice environment and rank my favorite artists based on how good they were compared to their contemporaries. Front-end is my biggest enemy so everything good about it is AI and everything bad is my fault. I'm sorry for that.
